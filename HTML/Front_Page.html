Mission/Vission


MLA is entirely committed to the development of an authentic Christian-Filipino, equipped with moral and socio-cultural values.

The school therefore adheres to the provision set forth in the Philippine Constitution which states “All educational institutions,

shall aim to inculcate patriotism and nationalism, foster love of humanity, respect for human rights, appreciation of the role of the

national heroes in the historical development of the country, teach the rights and duties of citizenship, strengthen ethical and

spiritual values, develop moral character and personal discipline, encourage critical and creative thinking, broaden scientific

and technological knowledge and provide vocational efficiency.”


Towards the realization of its ends, MLA shall offer a curriculum designed and enriched to develop and instill in all its students

knowledge, skills, interest, and habits essential to his meaningful development as worthy member of the Church,

the State, the School and the Home.


Historical Background

"Mona Lisa Academy (MLA), founded in 1993 by Mrs. Mona Lisa U. Acosta, offers Nursery to Secondary education.

and tech-forward, MLA provides comprehensive computer literacy programs. Recognized for its quality education,

Elementary Course gained full government recognition in 1995, followed by the Secondary Course in 2007. Today,

MLA boasts modern facilities and a commitment to excellence."
